SAP FSCM & Working Capital

Optimizing enterprise liquidity through intelligent Credit, Dispute, and Collections management.

Executive Summary

Cash flow is the lifeblood of the enterprise. I architect SAP FSCM (Financial Supply Chain Management) solutions that optimize working capital by automating the dispute-to-resolution cycle and providing data-driven credit risk assessments within the S/4HANA landscape.

Architectural Pillars

Credit Management

Utilizing real-time scoring to automate credit limits and mitigate bad debt risk.

Dispute Management

Streamlining workflows for billing discrepancies to reduce time-to-resolution.

Collections

Implementing prioritized worklists based on risk, value, and aging categories.

Strategic Impact

By reducing DSO (Days Sales Outstanding) and improving the customer experience through transparent billing and dispute handling, I directly impact the company’s bottom-line liquidity. My architecture ensures that the credit office is a strategic partner to the sales organization, not a bottleneck.
