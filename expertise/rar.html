SAP RAR & Revenue Compliance

Automating IFRS 15 and ASC 606 compliance for complex, high-volume global enterprises.

Executive Summary

I bridge the gap between financial regulations and technical automation. By architecting SAP RAR solutions, I help global enterprises navigate the complexities of IFRS 15 and ASC 606, ensuring that revenue is recognized accurately without manual intervention.

Architectural Pillars

1

Performance Obligations (POBs)

Automating the identification and grouping of obligations across Sales (SD), Billing (BRIM), and external sources for complex contract structures.

2

Transaction Price Allocation

Implementing Standalone Selling Price (SSP) logic and relative-price allocation models for multi-element arrangements and bundles.

3

Fulfillment & Recognition

Designing event-based and time-based revenue triggers that provide audit-ready transparency from sub-ledger to the General Ledger.

Strategic Impact

My implementations shorten the month-end close cycle and eliminate the "spreadsheet risk" typically associated with complex revenue recognition compliance. I provide a single source of truth for auditors and finance leadership.
