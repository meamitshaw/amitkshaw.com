Beyond the world of SAP architecture, I am a passionate **Model Railroader**. Much like designing a complex ERP landscape, model railroading requires a deep understanding of system integration, electrical engineering, and meticulous design.

### The Art of Miniaturization
My focus is on **HO Scale** layouts, where I combine historical accuracy with modern DCC (Digital Command Control) systems. Building a layout is essentially an "Implementation Project" that involves:

* **Civil Engineering:** Designing track plans, grades, and radii to ensure smooth operations.
* **Electrical Architecture:** Implementing bus wiring and power districts to manage multiple locomotives simultaneously.
* **System Integration:** Utilizing DCC technology to control speed, lighting, and realistic sound effects across the fleet.

---

### My Collection & Layout Features
* **Locomotive Fleet:** A curated collection of highly detailed steam and diesel engines.
* **Custom Scenery:** Hand-crafted landscapes, from industrial urban centers to rural mountain passes.
* **Operational Realism:** Using switch lists and car forwarding systems to simulate real-world logistics and freight movement.

> "In model railroading, as in SAP, success lies in the details. Every wire, every piece of track, and every line of code must work in harmony to create a functional system."
