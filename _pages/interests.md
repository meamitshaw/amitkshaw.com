### Model Railroading & Systems Design
My interest in model railroading (HO Scale) is an extension of my professional philosophy. Both require:
* **Complex Integration:** Merging electrical, mechanical, and digital systems.
* **Architectural Precision:** Designing layouts that balance aesthetic and operational efficiency.
* **Logic Systems:** Implementing DCC (Digital Command Control) to manage multi-locomotive traffic.
