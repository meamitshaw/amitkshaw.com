### Systems Thinking in Miniature
While my professional life is dedicated to orchestrating multi-billion dollar SAP landscapes, my personal passion is found in the meticulous world of **HO-Scale Model Railroading**.

To me, a model railroad is more than a hobby; it is a complex system that mirrors the challenges of Enterprise Architecture.

#### Why Model Railroading?
* **Complex Integration:** Just as SAP BRIM must integrate with external tax engines and ledgers, a model railroad requires the seamless integration of mechanical trackwork, electrical wiring, and digital command systems.
* **The Logic of Movement:** Implementing **DCC (Digital Command Control)** allows for the simultaneous operation of multiple locomotives—a direct parallel to managing multi-threaded data flows in high-volume billing systems.
* **Precision and Scalability:** Building a layout requires a "Big Picture" vision (the Enterprise view) balanced with extreme attention to minute detail (the Solution view).

#### Parallel Disciplines

| :--- | :--- |
| Business Process Flow | Mainline Traffic Routing |
| Data Integrity | Electrical Continuity |
| Modular Configuration | Scenic Layout Modules |
| System Performance | Rolling Stock Reliability |
