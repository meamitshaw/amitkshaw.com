### A Foundation in Financial Integrity
My career began over **13 years ago** with a deep dive into the world of **Auditing and Finance**. This foundational period was more than just a job; it was a masterclass in financial logic, regulatory compliance, and the "language of the CFO." Understanding how every penny must be accounted for shaped my philosophy as a consultant: technical systems are only as good as the financial truth they produce.

### The SAP Transformation (2015 – Present)
In **2015**, I pivoted my domain expertise into the SAP ecosystem. Over the last 8+ years, I have specialized in the most complex financial modules, including **BRIM, RAR, and FSCM**. 

I didn't just learn to configure these modules; I learned to architect them. My auditing background allowed me to approach SAP implementations with a unique perspective—ensuring that high-volume billing (BRIM) and complex revenue recognition (RAR) aren't just technically functional, but audit-proof.

### The Path to Enterprise Architecture
Currently, I operate at the vital intersection of **Senior Solution Architect** and **Enterprise Strategist**. While my "hands-on" mastery remains sharp, my focus has evolved toward:
* **Ecosystem Scalability:** Ensuring SAP landscapes support long-term business growth.
* **Risk Mitigation:** Aligning technical architecture with global standards like IFRS 15 and ASC 606.
* **Strategic Roadmap Design:** Moving beyond single-module solutions to enterprise-wide financial transformations.

I am actively pursuing my evolution into **Enterprise Architecture**, treating every project and publication as a step toward mastering the orchestration of complex business landscapes.

### Author & Enthusiast
I believe that true mastery is demonstrated by the ability to teach. Through my **Library** of articles and upcoming e-books, I aim to mentor other architects in navigating the "Lead-to-Cash" journey.

Outside of work, I am a dedicated **Model Railroader** (HO Scale). I find that the meticulous planning, electrical precision, and systems-level thinking required for a model railroad are the perfect analog to the architectural precision I bring to SAP.
