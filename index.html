The Architect's Story

Bridging Financial Integrity with Digital Innovation.

With over 13 years of professional experience, my journey began in the rigorous world of Auditing and Taxation within a Chartered Accountant firm. This foundational decade in finance shaped my perspective: I don't just see code or configurations; I see the financial statements and regulatory compliance that drive a business.

As a Certified SAP Solution Architect, I specialize in architecting complex, end-to-end "Lead-to-Cash" ecosystems. My expertise lies at the intersection of SAP BRIM, RAR, and FSCM. I have a proven track record of leading global digital transformations for high-volume sectors including Telecommunications, SaaS, and Utilities.

Beyond technical delivery, I am a passionate advocate for Clean Core principles and knowledge sharing. Whether I am optimizing high-volume billing through multithreading or ensuring IFRS-15 compliance via BRF+ logic, my goal is always to deliver scalable, audit-ready solutions that future-proof the enterprise.
