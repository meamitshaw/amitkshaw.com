### Executive Summary
As a BRIM Specialist, I design "Lead-to-Cash" cycles that handle massive transaction volumes while maintaining financial integrity. My architecture ensures that business model innovation—shifting from products to services—is supported by a robust, automated engine.

### Architectural Pillars
* **SOM (Subscription Order Management):** Designing complex product catalogs and bundle logic to capture multi-tier subscriptions.
* **CC (Convergent Charging):** Implementing sub-second rating logic for millions of consumption records.
* **CI (Convergent Invoicing):** Aggregating diverse billing streams into a single, transparent customer invoice.
* **FI-CA (Contract Accounting):** Mass-processing sub-ledger transactions with automated clearing and dunning.

### Strategic Impact
I enable organizations to scale subscription models globally, reducing revenue leakage through automated reconciliation between the technical mediation layer and the financial ledger.
